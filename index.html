WHAT IS PIXEL?


Project Innovation & exploration in CS Education and Learning (PIXEL 2025) is an annual grand showcase of innovative tech projects by junta final-year computer science students, organized by the Computer Science Society in collaboration with the , junta Anantapur


PIXEL 2025 is an extension of the Final Year Projects (FYP) evaluation, allowing students to exhibit their projects and receive valuable feedback from industry professionals. This year, PIXEL is back in hybrid mode with more innovative and groundbreaking tech projects.
